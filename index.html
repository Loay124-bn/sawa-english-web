About Sawa English

An app built around how people actually learn

Sawa English was created for learners who want steady, visible progress without overwhelming grammar charts or endless flashcard decks. Every lesson is short by design, so it fits into a coffee break, a commute, or the few quiet minutes before bed.

Behind the simple interface is a structured path through vocabulary, grammar, listening and speaking — each one reinforcing the others, so what you learn actually sticks.

  • Short, focused daily lessons
  • Clear progress tracking
  • Designed for real-world conversation
