Privacy Policy

This page explains how Sawa English handles data, advertising, and permissions within the application.

Data Collection Advertisements Internet Connection Permissions Third-Party Services Children's Privacy Changes Contact
Last updated: July 4, 2026

We respect the privacy of our users and are committed to protecting their information while using this application.

1 Data Collection

This application does not require account creation or user login. We do not directly collect personal information such as names, email addresses, or phone numbers.

2 Advertisements

This application contains advertisements provided by third-party advertising partners. These partners may use technologies such as cookies, device identifiers, or general usage data to deliver more relevant advertisements. Any data collected is handled according to the privacy policies of the respective advertising providers.

3 Internet Connection

The application can be used both online and offline. Some features and advertisements may require an internet connection to function properly.

4 Permissions

The application does not request unnecessary permissions. Any permissions used are required solely for the proper operation of the application's features.

5 Third-Party Services

The application may use third-party services, including advertising providers, which may collect limited technical information in accordance with their own privacy policies.

6 Children's Privacy

This application is intended for educational purposes and does not knowingly collect personal information from children. If any such information is discovered to have been collected unintentionally, appropriate steps will be taken to remove it.

7 Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time when necessary. Any changes will be posted on this page, and continued use of the application constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8 Contact Us

If you have any questions regarding this Privacy Policy, please contact us at sawa.english.app@gmail.com.
